Who I Am...

I'm Chad Blincoe, a web designer and developer based in Lakeland, FL who thrives on turning ideas into clean, purposeful digital experiences. I've always been drawn to the art of creating — whether it's building a website from the ground up, designing a brand's visual identity, or crafting graphics that tell a story. That passion for creation is what drives everything I do.

What keeps me hooked on this craft is the problem solving. There's nothing quite like digging into a challenge and finding the solution that makes everything fall into place — that moment when it all clicks is what pushes me to keep growing.

My background spans front-end development, graphic design, and digital marketing. I'm currently pursuing a Web Design certification at Hillsborough Community College, and every project I take on is an opportunity to learn something new and deliver work I'm genuinely proud of.
